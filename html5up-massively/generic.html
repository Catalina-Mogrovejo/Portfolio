About Me

Hi, I’m Catalina, a future financial data analyst with a unique journey and a strong foundation in problem-solving and working with data.
I began my career as an office dispatcher at an appliance repair company from 2018 to 2021, where I coordinated service calls and managed customer interactions. I then spent a year as an over-the-road truck driver, which sharpened my time management, planning, and problem-solving skills. From 2022 to 2025, I returned to the appliance repair company as a technician, gaining hands-on experience with service operations and internal workflows. In 2025, I transitioned back into the office dispatcher role, where I continue to manage schedules, track performance, and support daily business operations.
Throughout these roles, I’ve developed strong spreadsheet skills using Excel and Google Sheets to organize data, track jobs, and improve efficiency. I also completed a computer applications course that helped strengthen my technical foundation.
Now, I’m earning my Google Data Analytics Certificate through the Merit America program and expanding my skills in SQL, data visualization, and analytics. I’m especially drawn to financial data analysis because I enjoy identifying trends, solving problems, and helping businesses make smart, informed decisions.
